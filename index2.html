Scientific Research Measurement Data recorded off the coast(s) of Australia, New Zealand, Indonesia, Papua New Guinea, Philippines, Fiji, New Caledonia, Solomon Islands, Vanuatu.

IMOS - Argo Profiles. This data is collected by a combination of drifting subsurface profiling float in the Atlantic Ocean, Southern Ocean, Indian Ocean, Arabian Sea, Great Australian Bight SA/WA, Tasman Sea, Bass Strait TAS/VIC, Coral Sea, Pacific Ocean, Philippine Sea, Celebes Sea, East China Sea, Bismarck Sea off the coastlines(s) by Integrated Marine Observing System (IMOS).

The data sets are useful for scientific and/or academic research and are free to download from the IMOS Portal.
